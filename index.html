PHASE 1 · STEP 1
CV Upload

Admin uploads CV files (single or bulk).

  • Formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX
  • Scanned CVs supported
PHASE 1 · STEP 2
Text Extraction

The system determines whether the CV contains readable text.

  • If text-based → direct extraction
  • If scanned → OCR applied
PHASE 1 · STEP 3
AI CV Parsing

Extracted text is sent to an AI model for understanding.

  • Name, phone, email
  • Education and experience
  • Skills and location
  • Age only if explicitly mentioned

AI Used: OpenAI GPT-4 / GPT-4o

PHASE 1 · STEP 4
Candidate Profile Creation

A structured candidate profile is generated and stored.

  • Status: Unassigned
  • Searchable and filterable

PHASE 1 · STEP 5–6
Job Posts & Matching

Admin creates job posts separately.

  • Title, skills, experience
  • AI calculates match percentage

Example: Backend Developer – 86% match

PHASE 1 · STEP 9
Email Automation

Email is triggered only after job assignment.

From: hr@company.com

Method: SMTP (Zoho / Gmail / Outlook)

Template:
“You are shortlisted for Backend Developer role.”

PHASE 1 · STEP 9–10
WhatsApp Messaging

Uses official WhatsApp Business API.

Template:
“This job post is for Backend Developer. Are you interested?”

Candidate replies are captured and logged.

PHASE 2 · STEP 14
Automated Interview Call

Triggered only after explicit consent and admin approval.

Call API: Twilio / Exotel

Script:
“Your interview is scheduled for 15 Jan at 11 AM. Press 1 to confirm.”
